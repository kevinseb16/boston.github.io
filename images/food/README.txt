ADD YOUR OWN RESTAURANT PHOTOS (e.g. screenshots from Google reviews)
=====================================================================

Each restaurant gallery automatically looks for up to 4 photos in THIS folder.
Name them:   <restaurant>-1.jpg , <restaurant>-2.jpg , <restaurant>-3.jpg , <restaurant>-4.jpg
(.jpeg and .png also work.)

Tip: slots 1–3 are best for FOOD shots, slot 4 for the AMBIANCE / interior.
If a file isn't there, that slot falls back to a themed photo — so you can add
just one or two and it still looks complete. Upload this "images" folder with the HTML.

HOW TO GRAB A GOOGLE REVIEW PHOTO:
  1. Open the restaurant in Google Maps (use the "Google photos" button on its card).
  2. Click a photo you like  ->  right-click  ->  "Save image as..."
  3. Rename it to match the list below and drop it in this folder.
